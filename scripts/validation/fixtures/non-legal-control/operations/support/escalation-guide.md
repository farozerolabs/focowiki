# Customer Support Escalation Guide

Support agents escalate an issue when it affects multiple customers, causes data loss, blocks a critical workflow, or requires a production change.

The escalation includes the customer impact, reproduction steps, timestamps, request identifiers, and troubleshooting already completed. Credentials and private customer content must be removed.

Service incidents continue under the [Incident Response Runbook](../on-call/incident-response.md).
