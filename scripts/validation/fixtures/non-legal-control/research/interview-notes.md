# Workspace Setup Interview Notes

Participants understood workspace roles after seeing a concrete permission example. Several participants expected data connections to be tested before the first saved view was published.

The most common setup delay came from waiting for an owner to grant access. Teams with a written onboarding checklist completed setup more consistently.

The product concepts referenced by participants are described in [Atlas Workspace Overview](../product/product-overview.md).
